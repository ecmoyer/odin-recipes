Hummus

Photo by Nicholas Barbaros on Unsplash

Description

This creamy hummus can be used as a dip for veggies or crackers, or enjoyed as part of a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 1 19 oz can of chickpeas
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1 large or 2 small cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. Drain out half of the liquid from the can of chickpeas into a bowl.
  2. Pour the chickpeas with their remaining liquid into a blender.
  3. Add salt, lemon juice, tahini, and crushed garlic to the blender.
  4. Blend on high until smooth. Add the set-aside chickpea liquid as needed.
