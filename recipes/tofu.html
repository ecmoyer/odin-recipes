Fried Tofu with Tamari and Sesame Oil

Photo by Christina Deravedisian on Unsplash

Description

This recipe for fried tofu is quick and easy to make, and is a great introduction to tofu.

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp powdered ginger
  • Vegetable oil or other cooking oil
  • 2-3 Tbsp tamari
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil

Steps

  1. Slice the block of tofu in half lengthwise, then slice each half into approximately 1/4 to 3/8 inch thick rectangles.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the flour and the powdered ginger.
  3. Coat all sides of each tofu rectangle in the flour and ginger mixture and place on a plate.
  4. Place a medium-sized frying pan on the stove on medium high heat. Add vegetable oil (or other cooking oil) to the pan.
  5. Once the oil is hot, add the tofu to the pan.
  6. Fry the tofu for approximately 3 minutes, or until just starting to brown, then flip the tofu and fry again until beginning to brown, approximately 2 minutes.
  7. Once the tofu is light brown on both sides, lower the heat to as low as possible, then pour the tamari and sesame oil over the tofu.
  8. Remove from heat, and serve.
